Beef Stroganoff

Description

Beef stroganoff is a noodle dish with ground beef in a creamy sauce

Ingredients

  • Egg noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Mushroom soup or cream
  • Garlic powder
  • Sour cream
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef
  2. Boil egg noodles until done
  3. Add soup and garlic powder to beef and simmer 10 minutes
  4. After condensed and thickened, add noodles and sour cream
  5. Season to taste with salt and pepper
