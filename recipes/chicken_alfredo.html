Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken alfredo is a dish with pasta and grilled
chicken coated in a parmesean cream sauce

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Fettucini Pasta
  • Parmesean
  • Heavy Cream
  • Garlic
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Boil water for pasta
  2. Butterfly chicken breast and season
  3. Heat pan to medium and cook until done
  4. Put pasta boiling
  5. Lighty fry garlic in residue of chicken
  6. Deglaze with cream and stir
  7. Put in parmesean
  8. Strain noodles and put in cream, as well as a small amount of pasta water
  9. Stir for a while
  10. Plate with sliced chicken
